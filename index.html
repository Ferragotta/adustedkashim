About KMT

Kashim Musa Tumsah (KMT) is a man whose legacy stretches across multiple domains of life: law, diplomacy, business, and humanitarianism. His academic roots were nurtured in Maiduguri, where he attended the Shehu Garbai Primary School. His secondary education at the prestigious Kings College, Lagos, set him on the path to excellence. A highly gifted individual, KMT excelled academically throughout his life, earning top honors in both undergraduate and graduate courses. His journey began with his studies at the University of Maiduguri where he obtained a degree in law.

His commitment to excellence continued at the Nigerian Law School, where he was recognized as the best student from the Northern region. This excellence followed him to international academia, where he earned a Master's degree in Resources Law at the University of Dundee, UK. His specialization in oil and gas law played a critical role in shaping the discourse around transboundary oil reserves, particularly in the joint development zone between Nigeria and São Tomé and Príncipe.

KMT's professional trajectory has been nothing short of inspiring. He worked as a Senior Legal Officer for BP Exploration, which saw him play a key role in the exploration and management of natural resources. His expertise in oil and gas law led him to pivotal positions including legal adviser for multinational oil companies. He served as the Non-Executive Director of NNPC and contributed to several international peace-building efforts in energy diplomacy. His business ventures in oil and gas have been instrumental in the development of the Gulf of Guinea region. Through his company, Gulf of Guinea Consulting Ltd., KMT made significant contributions to the development of energy resources, both in Africa and globally.
